socket.error: [Errno 32] Broken pipe错误的原因
妙音 posted @ 2014-01-26 15:47 in python with tags

linux下的socket程序，尝试发送消息到已关闭的socket上，会出现此错误.

示例代码 服务端server.py

 1 #encoding=utf-8
 2 import socket
 3 import time
 4  
 5  
 6 def main():
 7     sock = socket.socket(socket.AF_INET, socket.SOCK_STREAM)
 8     sock.bind(('0.0.0.0', 8050))
 9     sock.listen(5)
10  
11     client_sock, address = sock.accept()
12     while True:
13         msg = client_sock.recv(1024)
14         print msg
15         client_sock.send('from server: %s' % msg)
16         time.sleep(1)
17     client_sock.close()
18     sock.close()
19  
20  
21 if __name__ == '__main__':
22     main()

客户端client.py

 1 #encoding=utf-8
 2 import socket
 3 import time
 4  
 5  
 6 def main():
 7     sock = socket.socket(socket.AF_INET, socket.SOCK_STREAM)
 8     sock.connect(('0.0.0.0', 8050))
 9     while True:
10         sock.send('hi')
11         print sock.recv(1024)
12         time.sleep(1)
13     sock.close()
14  
15  
16 if __name__ == '__main__':
17     main()

运行server.py和client.py，然后中断client.py会出现上面错误。
